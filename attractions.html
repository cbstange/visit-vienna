Attractions

Karlzplatz

Karsplatz is centrally located in the heart of Vienna and is a main public transportation hub.


Karsplatz is home to the iconic Karlskirche - a Baroque church dating back to the 18th century. In front of the church there are often seasonal markets and shows.

Schönbrunn Palace

Dating back to the 17th century, Schönbrunn is now one of Europe's most striking examples of Baroque arcitecture.


The palace grounds are open to the public for free, however a tour is highly recommended.

Naschmarkt

Naschmarkt is Vienna's most popular market, boasting over 120 different stalls and unique restaurants.


Naschmarkt is open 7 days a week with free public wifi.
