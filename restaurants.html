Restaurants

Café Central

Café Central is an iconic example of a classic Viennese coffee house with amazing pastries and authentic dishes.

Café Korb

Located in the 1st district in Vienna, Café Korb is known for its delicious coffee and for Kaiserschmarrn, a light and fluffy shredded pancake dish.

Palmenhaus

Brasserie Palmenhaus is perfect for weekend brunch and being inside of a botanic garden, there is definitely a jungle vibe.
